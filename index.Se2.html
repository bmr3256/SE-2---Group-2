Frequently Asked Questions

What ingredients do you use in your dog treats?
We use only natural, human-grade ingredients in all our treats. Each product lists specific ingredients on the packaging.
Are your dog treats suitable for all breeds and sizes?
Yes! Our treats come in a variety of sizes to accommodate dogs of all breeds, ages, and sizes. If you’re unsure, feel free to ask us for recommendations based on your pet's needs.
How should I store your dog treats?
To maintain freshness, store the treats in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. For long-term storage, some treats may be frozen—check the packaging for specific instructions.
Do the treats have a specific expiration date?
Yes, we make sure that the storage use of the treats is long term and we make fresh batches weekly.
How do we ensure the ingredients contained in the treats are allergen free?
We ensure transparency for your fur babies so the ingredients and health benefits are visible in the packaging.
How do I place an order?
To place an order, simply browse our products, select the treats you want, and click Add to Cart. Once you’re done shopping, proceed to checkout and follow the instructions to place your order.
Do I need to create an account to order?
Yes, an account is required to place an order. This helps us provide a smoother checkout experience, order tracking, and timely updates on your purchases.
What payment methods are available?
At the moment, we offer Cash on Delivery (COD) through J&T Express. This allows you to pay safely and conveniently once your order arrives.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
Orders can only be modified or canceled within a limited time after checkout. Please contact us as soon as possible so we can assist you before your order is processed.
How will I know if my order is confirmed?
Once your order is successfully placed, you will receive a confirmation via email with your order details.
