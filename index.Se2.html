Frequently Asked Questions

What ingredients do you use in your dog treats?
We use only natural, human-grade ingredients in all our treats. Each product lists specific ingredients on the packaging.
Are your dog treats suitable for all breeds and sizes?
Yes, our treats are designed to be suitable for dogs of all breeds and sizes. We offer different portion sizes to accommodate various dog sizes.
How should I store your dog treats?
To maintain freshness, store the treats in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. For long-term storage, some treats may be frozen—check the packaging for specific instructions.
Do the treats have a specific expiration date?
Yes, all our treats have expiration dates printed on the packaging. We recommend using treats before this date for optimal freshness and quality.
How do we ensure the ingredients contained in the treats are allergen free?
We clearly list all ingredients on our packaging. If your dog has specific allergies, please check the ingredient list before purchasing.
How do I create an account and place an order?
Click the user icon in the top right corner to sign up. Fill in your details, then browse our products and add them to your cart. Follow the checkout process to complete your order.
